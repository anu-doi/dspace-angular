This privacy notice will explain how The Australian National University uses the personal data we collect from you when you use this repository.

What data do we collect?

  • Univeristy ID number
  • Personal identification information (Name, Email address, Phone number)

How do we collect your data?

We collect and process data when you:

  • Register to use the ANU Open Research repository
  • Use or view the ANU Open Research Repository via your browser's cookies

How will we use your data?

We collect your data for the purposes of providing you access to deposit content and read restricted contnet within the ANU Open Research Repository

How can you have your data corrected?

If your data is incorrect, please contact us at repository.admin@anu.edu.au to have your data corrected.

Information on privacy at The Australian National University can be found at https://www.anu.edu.au/privacy
